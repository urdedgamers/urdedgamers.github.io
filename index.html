Fragfest

Call of Duty mp v1.5 mod. Heavy weapons alt fire = antitank rifle fire. Slightly raised weapon damage, almost all weapons have alt fire. In game paintings and posters have been re-skinned, don't get caught looking.

Rockets Mod for COD

This a small mod (50kb) that turns COD MP into a rocket fest. You only get the Panzerfaust and nades for weapons. Pistols have 0 ammo.






SNIPERS!

This is a mod for Call of Duty MP v1.5. In this mod you only get 3 weapons to choose from for each team. Your choice of an iron sight rifle or 2 different sniper rifles per team. You also get 2 smoke nades. Thats it. There are also 10 sniper related maps added for your enjoyment.
